# Upside Capital Markets — Design Brainstorm

## Design Brief
Commercial real estate capital markets firm based in Boston. Needs a permanent, professional website that conveys authority, expertise, and innovation in the CRE investment space.

---

## Selected Design: **Refined Institutional Elegance**

Chosen because it best matches the brand positioning of a Boston-based CRE capital markets firm: authoritative, trustworthy, editorial, and premium without being flashy. The warm white + forest green + antique gold palette communicates stability and growth — core values for investors and developers.
